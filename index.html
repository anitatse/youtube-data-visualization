Top 10 Trending Youtube Categories

By Anita Tse, Leo Lin, Michael Goh
In this visualization, we gather statistics of 352,971 Youtube videos selected from the top 10 categories across 10 different countries, and published between November 2017 and June 2018. Use the dropdown menu above to toggle between likes, comments, and views for all of these visualizations. The top 10 countries visualized here are Canada, Germany, France, Great Britain, India, Japan, Korea, Mexico, Russia, and United states, with the top 10 categories being Entertainment, People & Blogs, Music, News & Politics, Comedy, Sports, Film & Animation, Howto & Style, Gaming, and Science & Technology.
